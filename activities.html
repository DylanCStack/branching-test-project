Activities

Fjordington offers a wide range of activities both within the resort and in the surrounding landscape. Some of them include:

  • Skiing
  • Snowshoe hikes
  • Reindeer rides
  • Daily ferries through the fjords
  • Fishing
  • Whale-watching
  • And much more!

Contact our travel concierge to make arrangements or with any questions!
